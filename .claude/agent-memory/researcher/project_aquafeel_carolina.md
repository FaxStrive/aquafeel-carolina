AquaFeel Solutions (aquafeelsolutions.com) is a water treatment company headquartered in Wake Forest, NC (230 Capcom Ave, Ste 103). Founded 2007, CEO Cesar Angulo, DBA of Diamond Perfection Inc.

The aquafeel-carolina project is a NEW regional landing page targeting NC and SC. It is NOT yet live — we are building it.

**Why:** The company operates national franchise-style regional sites (Maryland, Virginia, Tri-State, Connecticut, etc.) and needs a dedicated Carolina site to capture local SEO and serve as a conversion-focused landing page for the Carolinas market.

**How to apply:** Always position the Carolina site as locally rooted (HQ is already in NC), use NC/SC-specific water quality data (Raleigh hard water, Charlotte PFAS, rural well water), and emphasize the company's genuine local presence vs. competitors that are truly from outside the region.

**Key products:**
1. Aquafeel Whole House System (~$7,990) — single tank, 4-stage, Vortech technology, lifetime media warranty, 25-year guarantee
2. Alkaline Water System (under-sink RO, pH 10.5, 5-stage + alkaline stage, NSF-58 certified)
3. Replacement filter kits

**Unique differentiators to always emphasize:**
- Vortech technology (3 plates, proprietary — no competitor has this)
- 25-year guarantee + lifetime media warranty (industry-leading)
- Made in USA
- 3+ years organic cleaning + personal care products included (unique bundle)
- BBB A+ since 2018
- Free in-home water test (valued $175–$250)
- 18+ years in business, HQ in Wake Forest NC

**Known competitive weaknesses (do NOT ignore):**
- Customer service complaints are real (maintenance costs not upfront, $390 service call not covered by warranty)
- Some markets have "scam" accusations from misleading sales tactics
- Carolina site copy must be transparent about maintenance costs and what the warranty covers
- Do NOT use "free government program" language

**Primary competitors in Carolinas:**
- Pure Life Carolinas (purelifecarolinas.com) — Rock Hill SC, same model, HIGH threat
- Kinetico Advanced Water Systems NC (kineticoadvancedwatersystems.com) — Garner NC, 40 years, HIGH credibility
- Progressive Water Solutions (cleanyourwater.com) — Durham, local family business
- A&E Water Conditioning (aandewater.com) — Fuquay-Varina, 30 years local
